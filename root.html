Mobile app for travelling
with public transport

I came up with an idea of designing the public transportation app after I visited Berlin for a vacation in November 2019. I was impressed by its public transportation system and how easily one can commute through the city.

To structure and better understand information, I started the project by defining two main target audience groups: tourists and locals. Thus, the app must be efficient for both of them. I used user interviews, user stories and customer journey mapping for that.

I listed main challenges they might be facing which gave me insights what and how citizens and tourists look for in the cities while using public transport.

  • How can I get to the desired destination

    Help user to understand where he is now and find point
    of destination.

  • Help me to choose the best route

    Give different options how user can choose a route:
    quickest, no transfers etc.

  • How can I visit prominent touristic spots?

    Design a section with hints for sightseeing.

  • I want to go to another city

    In the app people can book a ticket for a train.
